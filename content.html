Mental health

This comprehensive guide to understanding mental health provides an in-depth understanding of the topic,
including its definition, common disorders, coping strategies, and resources for seeking help.
Mental health refers to an individual's emotional, psychological, and social well-being, enabling them to cope with stress,
work productively, and contribute to their communities. Common mental health disorders include anxiety disorders,
depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD). To cope,
individuals should seek professional help, practice self-care, build a support system, maintain a healthy lifestyle,
and practice mindfulness. Seeking help is essential, and resources like the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline,
Crisis Text Line, and SAMHSA National Helpline can provide personalized support and treatmentÂ options.
